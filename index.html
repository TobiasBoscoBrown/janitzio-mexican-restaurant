Our story

One family, one kitchen, since 2006.

Janitzio takes its name from a little island in Michoacan, and that is the cooking you will find here. For more than twenty years the same family has run this corner of Caldwell, serving the tacos, enchiladas, fajitas, pozole and seafood they grew up on.

Regulars will tell you not to judge the place by the outside. Step in and it is warm, clean and full of color, with murals on the walls and a salsa people swear is the best in the valley. The welcome is as genuine as the food.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Where are you located?

We are at 313 S Kimball Ave in Caldwell, Idaho. Look for the orange building with the gold Janitzio sign and the blue mural along the bottom.

What are your hours?

Monday to Wednesday we are open 11am to 8pm, and Thursday and Friday 11am to 9pm. We are closed Saturday and Sunday.

Do you do takeout and delivery?

Yes. You can dine in, call ahead for takeout, or have it delivered. For larger family or group orders, just give us a call.

What is Janitzio known for?

Traditional Michoacan cooking, our chile rellenos and bandera enchiladas, a full lineup of seafood, and the house salsa folks drive across the valley for.

How long have you been open?

One Caldwell family has owned and cooked here since 2006, more than twenty years.

Hungry for the real thing?

Come sit down, call ahead, or order delivery. Twenty years of Michoacan cooking, and a seat at the table with your name on it.
